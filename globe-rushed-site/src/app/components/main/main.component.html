Get to know Globe Rushed

Globe rushed is a fast paced geography guessing game that drops you somewhere in the world and gives you a handful of seconds to guess where you're at before your next leap. Guess right and you'll earn badges as well as a spot on our leaderboards. You can rank yourself amongst your friends or amongst our global community! Register now or keep reading if you want to know more about Globe Rushed!
