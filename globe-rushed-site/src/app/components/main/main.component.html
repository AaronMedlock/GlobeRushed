Get to know Globe Rushed

Globe rushed is a fast paced geography guessing game that drops you somewhere in the world and gives you a handful of seconds to guess where you're at before your next leap. Guess right and you'll earn badges as well as a spot on our leaderboards. You can rank yourself amongst your friends or amongst our global community! Register now or keep reading if you want to know more about Globe Rushed!

To get started, simply create an account and login. Once you do, select your game mode and begin making leaps across the globe. You'll have a handful of seconds to choose from a handful of options based on your surroundings using Google Streetview.

Choose correctly and you'll be awarded points that allow you to rank up and place on our leaderboards. Having trouble with a particular location? You can spend some of those points to get a hint that might make your selection a little easier.

Simply log into your account and at the bottom of your friend's leaderboard you'll see a button to add a new friend using their username.

To see how you rank globally, simply visit our leaderboards. If you want to see how you rank against your friends, simply log in and you'll find a customized leaderboard with your friend's progress.
